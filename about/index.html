Digital Disruption Matrix 2026

The Digital Disruption Matrix is a yearly barometer from the ESSEC Digital Disruption Chair designed for leaders seeking clarity on how emerging technologies reshape industries.

Who this is for

  • Current and future global leaders making technology portfolio decisions
  • Innovation and transformation teams planning adoption roadmaps
  • Executives and investors tracking cross-sector disruption intensity
  • Operational and risk teams preparing for near-term capability shifts

How to interpret the matrix

  • Each cell maps one technology × one industry intersection on a 0-100 disruption scale.
  • Technology columns reveal cross-sector momentum while industry rows show domain exposure and readiness.
  • Darker intensity indicates stronger disruption potential in the near-to-medium term.
  • The model combines market signals, publications, patents, and executive survey responses into calibrated disruption scores.
  • Signal pillars include ecosystem maturity, innovation intensity, momentum, and professional perception.
  • Scores are normalized to keep comparisons consistent across all technology and industry intersections.

Editorial and institutional context

The framework is maintained by the ESSEC Digital Disruption Chair with support from ecosystem partners, and is structured for yearly refreshes without changing the core templates.
